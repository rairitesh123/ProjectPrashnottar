Overview of Prashnottar DLP Course

Given the growing demand of Distance Education for engineering and even medical; Prashnottar, the pioneer of coaching institutes for competitive exams, started Prashnottar Distance Learning courses for NEET-UG and IIT-JEE with the aim to help students who are not able to join classroom classes get the expertise & proficiency of Prashnottar at the comfort of their home.
Today, Prashnottar has successfully established a strong foothold in Distance Education courses with a large number of students enrolled in distance education for JEE Main, Advanced & NEET-UG (engineering and medical) from across the world. Following its principles of incorporating the latest and result-driven methods to improve the learning experience for students, Prashnottar Distance Learning Programme and its Best DLP programme for NEET and IIT JEE follows a carefully designed study approach.
The study material as provided to students covers the comprehensive syllabus and follows the latest exam pattern. Along with Distance Learning for Pre-Engineering and Distance Learning Medical courses in India, Prashnottar also offers Distance Learning course for Olympiads.
Distance Learning Program (DLP) is a new platform specially designed for the students who are unable to take guide from experts due to their living locations. This help students to learn by expert teachers from their home over internet. In this program the teachers will guide their students via recorded videos lectures & study material which will be provided to students via post & as per student convenience live streaming doubt sessions will be taken and a virtual platform will be provided for basis doubts with 24X7 support.
