About us

Learning with Love and Laughter

It is an organization which aims to build a better India by providing high quality Education to the students of our country. The vision of the organization is to create a platform for the students where students coming from any background irrespective of their mother tongue they can learn explore their future with us by using our different learning program.

  • We will develop a passion for learning in our students to compete with the world So that no one can cease you to grow.
  • Using knowledge graphs, the program adapts and creates personalised learning journeys for students.



This vision derives itself from the belief that Education has the power to transform and change a person's life and hence the country's future. Our expertise lies in Medical & Engineering entrance exam and that's where we begin. In the modern fast changing world, the actual motive of providing education is shifted from creating future leader to the "Business" whereas we mainly focus on the "Student".
In this model the Student remains as the major element of focus in everything we do. The prospectus includes Teaching methodology, Infrastructure and further strategies. We at Prashnottar believe that basic years which decides the future of the individual students is IX, X, XI, XII. We at Prashnottar explore our each student’s capabilities and suggest parents by doing separate counselling with student and parents what comprises best for student future.
